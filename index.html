Beachfront Vacation House

A Reel Deal is a beautiful beachfront vacation rental house located in The Plantation on St. George Island, Florida. Just 22 steps from the beach and spectacular views of the Gulf of Mexico, A Reel Deal is a 4-bedroom, 3-bath house that sleeps 13. It features 2 beachfront Master bedroom suites, 2 Queen bedrooms and a separate upstairs living area that sleeps 5, perfect for kids of all ages. There are also 2 Pack 'n Plays and safety gate for the little ones. Fish on the beach, sunbathe at the private pool or enjoy the gorgeous views and the cool sea breeze from either of the two covered decks. When you're ready for a stroll on the beach or swim in the blue-green Gulf waters, just follow your private boardwalk to the beach.

With 2 separate living areas, 2 covered decks, private beach access, and a private pool, there is room for everyone to enjoy their vacation as they please in this gorgeous house.

The Plantation also offers pickleball and tennis courts, a family pool, a work-out facility, and walking / bike trails.
