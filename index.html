A Reel Deal

Beachfront Vacation House

A Reel Deal is a beautiful beachfront vacation rental house located in The Plantation on St. George Island, Florida. Just 22 steps from the beach and spectacular views of the Gulf of Mexico, A Reel Deal is a 4-bedroom, 3-bath house that sleeps 13. It features 2 beachfront Master bedroom suites, 2 Queen bedrooms and a separate upstairs living area that sleeps 5, perfect for kids of all ages. There are also 2 Pack 'n Plays and a safety gate for the little ones. Fish on the beach, sunbathe at the private pool or enjoy the gorgeous views and the cool sea breeze from either of the two covered decks. When you're ready for a stroll on the beach or a swim in the blue-green Gulf waters, just follow your private boardwalk to the beach.

With 2 separate living areas, 2 covered decks, private beach access, and a private pool, there is room for everyone to enjoy their vacation as they please in this gorgeous house.

The Plantation also offers pickleball and tennis courts, a family pool, a work-out facility, and walking / bike trails.

Bird's Eye View

St. George Island Florida is a beautiful 22-mile barrier island in the Gulf of Mexico and is home to some of the most stunning views of the Gulf in all of the Sunshine State. Uncrowded beaches are perfect for family-friendly activities—from swimming, fishing, kayaking and paddleboarding in the clear gulf waters to shelling, castle-building and relaxing on the sugar white sand. Nature lovers can venture over to the Apalachicola Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve or out to St. George Island’s beautiful bay marshes to enjoy extraordinary wildlife and stunning views of the sunset. The Plantation on St. George Island, where this beautiful home is located, is a coastal gated community with five miles of pristine, white-sand beaches, the famous Apalachicola Bay, towering pines, and incredible birds like peregrine falcons and bald eagles. After a long day of fun and adventure, you can relax and gaze with awe at the star-studded dark southern sky where the Milky Way is clearly visible.

Guys Trip

Planning a guys getaway or fishing trip? A Reel Deal is the perfect place for you. Located in The Plantation, a gated community on St. George Island, Florida, the house is just 22 steps to the beach and to some of the best fishing in all of Florida. Right from the beach, cast your line for Pompano, Red Fish, Whiting, Snapper, Flounder, Grouper, Sea Trout, Mackerel, and Tripletail just to name a few. Then clean your catch in the outside fish sink and grill it up on the outdoor gas grill for a delicious meal. Or go into town for dining and entertainment. St. George Island is also home to several great restaurants and bars with a wide array of delicious offerings and live music.
